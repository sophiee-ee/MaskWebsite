Windowed Face Mask - Purple

Features

Sanitized: Washed twice - cotton is pre-shrunk in the first wash, prior to making the mask, and again, once fully made.

Cleaning: Wash in soapy water - as this destroys the virus's outer skin. Hand wash only, no ironing. No microwaving, no machine wash. Air dry.

Tie backs vs elastic bands on ears: No pain tiebacks! Down each side of the mask we have a corded tie. You take the top two ties and lace them behind your head, and the bottom two ties and lace them behind your neck. All done! No more painful ears or losing hearing aids when removing or putting on the mask.

Anti-fogging tips: Dab inside of clear window with either a drop of dishsoap or shaving cream. Smear over whole inside window and wipe with damp cloth.

Adjustable fit: you can pull up the nose area, pull down the chin area, and squeeze the sides to get the best fit.
